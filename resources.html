Resources

A list of resources that may be helpful to you as an Olin student. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to a Peer Advocate and we will do our best to direct you to the right place.

Sexual Misconduct & Title IX Resources

For questions about the Title IX policy, a situation, or filing a Title IX report, contact the Title IX Coordinator Stephanie Milton. You can also reach out to the Deputy Title IX Coordinator Guilene Prepetit.

For questions about counseling, mental health, and sexual health resources contact Beth Grampetro.

Peer Advocates are always available to talk or if you need help reaching out (or if you are still deciding if you want to reach out).

Trans at Olin Information

Changing Olin name/email

Rae-Anne approves name and email changes. You can also reach out to Adva for information. It is a relatively simple one page form to get a name and email change, though there have been issues with people not following through - so make sure to follow up to make sure everyone does their job in getting your info changed.

Changing directory photo, pronouns, and/or name

Contact Miriam Stulin (Marketing and Communications Specialist) with the updated information. In the past, Rae-Anne and Seth of STAR could also be contacted, but contacting Miriam Stulin directly seems to be the quickest way to get information updated.
