Current PAs

Below are the current peer advocates with names, pronouns, emails, and a short bio. If you are unfamiliar with the peer advocate team members and unsure of who to contact, reach out to any peer advocate with any information you feel comfortable providing. We will do our best to connect you with the best match from our team.

Even if a topic is outside of a peer advocate's areas of experience, we are comfortable listening about most topics. We will let you know if we are not equipped or comfortable with a particular topic, and will direct you to someone who can help.
